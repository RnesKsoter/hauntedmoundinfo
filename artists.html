Artists

Sematary

Joined in 2019, Sematary created Haunted Mound alongside Ghost Mountain. He's the group's most active member, making beats since 2017-2018 but started making music at just eleven. With Ghost Mountain, he began dropping music in 2019, paving the way for the Haunted mound

Buckshot

joined in 2020,Buckshot is the first rapper signed to the Haunted mound. He used to make art for the Haunted mound, like the cover for Hundred acre wrist. He's Irish so he's not that active in comparison to others, who all live in the USA.

Turnabout

Joined in 2021, Turnabout is a rapper from utah and used to make songs in the hyperpop genre, but completely switched styles since joining

Hackle

Joined in 2021, Hackle is a rapper who became friends with sematary through instagram. He started to shoot the music videos for sematary, but eventually started to make music aswell. He's also heavily involved with the merch and makes sure everythings gets shipped correctly

Gonerville

Joined in 2021, Gonerville is a producer who produces alot for his friend Turnabout. Almost every Turnabout song is produced by Goner, but he also produces for others. Besides this he also has a verse on the Turnabout song: Play in your blood

Grimoire

Joined in 2021, Grimoire is a producer featured on the song peterbilt and had also produced songs on the Screaming forest album.

Oscar18

Joined in 2021, He produces alot of buckshot songs, as he is also from Ireland. He also had production credits on Screaming forest

Snuffer

Joined in 2021, Snuffer had the most prolific background in terms of music before joining. Being a producer called Slick Killa, now having changed his name produces songs for Haunted mound. However Snuffer deleted his instagram September 18th and removed his songs from streaming, so his future is unclear.

Former members

Ghost mountain

Joined in 2019 and left in 2021, Ghost mountain co-founded the Haunted mound label but took a step back to focus on his career in film. This step back turned into a full on retirement from music. He only has one solo song called: coil.

JJ Valhalla

Joined in 2021 and left in 2022, JJ Valhalla made alot of the designs for the merch, if not the most. He got kicked from the label after talking shit about Sematary.
